About

While working at the State Department, I graduated from Film School at American University.
I live in Los Angeles with my two greyhounds. I'm obsessed with Calvin and Hobbes, Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid, and some artsy shit that you've never heard of.

I also suck at golf and am going to have to caddie for Tom since I bet on Mike Trout to have a higher war than Ohtani over a five year period, and now Trout has some weird back condition I've never heard of.

While I love most aspects of the film process, I concentrate on screenwriting, specifically comedies. I will probably complain incessantly about this copy, but refuse to propose actual copy to provide here. I'll just make multiple jokes but not want to actually list my accomplishments because I'm weirdly ashamed for some reason, even though my writing is dope as shit and the world would love it if I was actually willing to put it out there. When will I start believing in myself as much as everyone else does?
